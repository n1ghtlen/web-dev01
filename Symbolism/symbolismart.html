The Art of Symbolism

History of Symbolism

Symbolism was an artistic movement most prominent in the late 19th century. The style is based on a dreamy, fantastical take of reality and was unsurprisingly a reaction to realism eras like Impressionism. Like the name suggests, symbols are often used to portay the inner world of the subject or the artist.
